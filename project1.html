Making personal finance
easy and manageable

The main objective of making this app was to help people to keep records of their savings.

Money Manager App let you save your incomes and expenses in an easy way so you can check them later on and make easy decisions regarding your financial plans, you can pick from several categories to organize your incomes or expenses and then make adjustments to reach your goals faster. With News you can search through millions of articles of any topic so you can plan your budget and future incomes or expenses.

Tech Stack

All the technologies used behind the app
  • Swift 5 - Programming Language used for writing Money Manager App
  • Firebase Authentication - For user authentication, with Google account and emails.
  • Core Data - For backing up users incomes and expenses data.
  • NSUserDefaults - For saving users data locally
  • Netwoking - use of Codable and URLSession to fetch data from NEWS API.
